home page

NXTCamView configures and controls the NXTCam produced by mindsensors. NXTCam is a camera sensor that provides real-time vision recognition to NXT robots. NXTCam uses simple colour recognition to detect objects in its view.

setting up colors to track

The main purpose of NXTCamView is to setup colors for tracking.  Think of this as teaching the NXTCam the colors of the objects you want to track.  Colors are setup in ranges that are uploaded and stored in the NXTCam.

To setup colors -

  1. Download, install and start NXTCamView on your PC
  2. Setup communication to the NXTCam via Tools\Options.  Often only the COMPort needs to be set.
  3. Make sure the NXTCam is plugged into one of your PCs USB port.  Connect to your NXTCam.
  4. Capture an image (or two) to check the focus and lighting conditions.
  5. Select some colors to track by clicking on the image you captured. Colors are shown as ranges of red, green and blue (min/max values of each). You can add or remove colors from a range by holding down the "CTRL" key or "SHIFT-CTRL" keys while clicking.
  6. Upload the color ranges into the NXTCam.
  7. Test your colors in the Tracking window to see what image blobs are returned.
  8. Disconnect NXTCamView from your NXTCam.

That's all its takes to get setup!
